    Star Org Labs¶

    Run your Star Citizen org like a pro — from Discord to a full web command center. Events, fleet, loot, mining, and org ops in one connected system.

    Open the app → Get started

    Why Star Org Labs?¶

    Running an org is a second job — scheduling ops, chasing signups, tracking who actually showed, splitting loot fairly, keeping the fleet and treasury straight. Star Org Labs pulls all of it into one connected system so you spend less time on spreadsheets and more time flying.

    •  Events & Operations

      Plan an op in minutes with a guided event wizard. Members sign up for roles and ships in Discord, you reuse templates for regular runs, track attendance & reliability over time, and settle the haul with the built-in Loot Hub.

    •  Fleet & Hangar

      Every member gets a Personal Hangar to register their ships, and leadership gets an Org Fleet Dashboard to see exactly what the org can field.

    •  Org Command

      A home base for leadership: member & server dashboards, personnel roster, org structure, a recruitment hub, support tickets, goals, guild points, treasury, and a public website CMS.

    •  Industrial

      Tools for the working fleet: mining sessions, a cargo optimizer, the RSIG decoder, a rock calculator, and work orders.

    •  Make it yours

      Theme the whole portal to your manufacturer of choice — Aegis, Drake, Origin, RSI, MISC, and more — or set an org-wide look for your members.

    •  Lives in Discord

      Signups, reminders, and pings happen right where your members already are. Your leadership commands from the web; your members never leave chat.

    Ready to launch?¶

    Getting started takes a few minutes and zero installs — sign in with Discord, add the bot, set up your org, and run your first op.
